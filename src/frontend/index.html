FLINT (Freshman Leaders in New Technology) is a FLO dedicated to cultivating a community of freshman and staff to develop each other into skilled leaders of the future.

Dedicated

Our goal is to create a community dedicated to each other and to upholding our core values. In order to get the best experience in any organization, you have to really get involved, so we want our members to do just that.

Open to Learning

We come from a variety of backgrounds and levels of experience, but there is always room to grow. We expect our members to learn from and teach eachother to make everyone better. This can be through our variety of workshops, build team meetings, and even impromptu study sessions.

Responsible

As an engineering organization, we are very aware of the time commitments of our members. That being said, we highly value the ability of our members to be responsible with their time and lifestyle, so they can be involved in our organization as much as possible.

Communicative

Communication is essential in every field, and in a cooperative environment like FLiNTs, communication can make the difference between a successful project and a dysfunctional team.

Inclusive

We strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome, and we expect all of our members to do the same.
