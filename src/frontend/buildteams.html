Build Teams

Showcasing the most innovative student engineering teams from universities across the country. These teams design, build, and compete with cutting-edge technology.

Flockheedmartin drone team

Building autonomous robots for competition and research. We specialize in AI, computer vision, and mechanical design for various robotics challenges.

Wet Bandits aquatics team

Engineering high-performance race cars for Formula SAE competitions. Focus on chassis design, engine optimization, and aerodynamics.

DIRT environmental team

Designing and piloting high-speed racing drones for collegiate competitions. Emphasis on flight control systems and aerodynamic optimization.

2025-2026

CMU Autonomous Vehicle Team

Developing self-driving vehicle technology for urban environments. Specializing in sensor fusion, machine learning, and real-time decision making.

UC Berkeley Space Team

Building satellites and rocket systems for space exploration missions. Focus on propulsion systems, orbital mechanics, and space communication.

Texas A&M Submarine Team

Designing autonomous underwater vehicles for marine research and competition. Expertise in underwater navigation, sonar systems, and marine engineering.

Virginia Tech Hyperloop

Pioneering next-generation transportation through magnetic levitation and vacuum tube technology. Focus on propulsion systems and pod design.
