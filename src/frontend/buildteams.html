2025-2026

CMU Autonomous Vehicle Team

Developing self-driving vehicle technology for urban environments. Specializing in sensor fusion, machine learning, and real-time decision making.

UC Berkeley Space Team

Building satellites and rocket systems for space exploration missions. Focus on propulsion systems, orbital mechanics, and space communication.
