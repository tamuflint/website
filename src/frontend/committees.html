Committees

Discover the various committees that drive FLINT's mission forward. Each committee plays a vital role in our organization's success and student development.

Marketing Committee

Manages FLINT's brand, social media presence, and promotional materials. Ensures effective communication with our community.

Service Committee

Oversees the overall direction and strategic planning of FLINT. Manages organizational goals and coordinates between all committees.

Event Planning Committee

Plans and executes all FLINT events, workshops, and social gatherings. Creates opportunities for networking and community building.

Professional Outreach Committee

Connects with industry partners, alumni, and other organizations. Builds relationships that benefit FLINT members and the community.

Internal Social Committee

Fosters community and camaraderie within FLINT through internal events, team building activities, and member engagement initiatives.

External Social Committee

Builds relationships with other organizations, universities, and communities through collaborative events and social initiatives.

Design Committee

Provides technical expertise and support for FLINT projects. Organizes workshops and training sessions for skill development.

Fundraising Committee

Manages FLINT's budget, fundraising efforts, and financial planning. Ensures sustainable funding for all organizational activities.
